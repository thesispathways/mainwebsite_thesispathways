About Us

Empowering students to explore original research and achieve their academic potential through expert mentorship.

Our Story

ThesisPathways was founded with a simple but powerful idea: students deserve the opportunity to explore original research and develop skills that go beyond the classroom. Our program connects high school and undergraduate students with mentors from top universities, guiding them through meaningful projects, fostering curiosity, and building confidence.

"Our mission is to help every student realize their academic potential and discover their path to success."

What We Offer

  • One-on-One Mentorship: Structured guidance from PhD-level mentors in STEM, humanities, and social sciences.
  • Original Research Projects: Students design and execute research projects, building skills in problem-solving, writing, and critical thinking.
  • Publication Opportunities: Students can publish their work in the Journal of Student Pathways to Research, a multidisciplinary platform for high school and undergraduate research.
  • Remote Flexibility: All mentorship is conducted online, allowing students across the U.S. to participate.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike traditional tutoring, ThesisPathways emphasizes mentorship through structured, student-led research projects. Every student has the chance to take ownership of a project and see it through to publication. Our mentors are PhD or MD graduates and candidates from top-tier universities, providing guidance that is both expert and personalized.

Our Values

  • Curiosity: Encouraging students to ask questions and explore ideas beyond the classroom.
  • Excellence: High standards in research, writing, and mentorship.
  • Independence: Students take ownership of their learning journey.
  • Accessibility: Providing mentorship and research opportunities to motivated students everywhere.
