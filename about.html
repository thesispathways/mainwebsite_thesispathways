About Us

Our Story

ThesisPathways was founded by a PhD graduate from Yale University. The company was started out of a passion for guiding curious students. Inspired by her own research journey and the amazing mentors she's had in her life, she wanted to create a program where high school and undergraduate students could explore original research, receive personalized mentorship, and develop skills that open doors to college and meaningful careers.

"Our mission is to help every student realize their academic potential and discover their path to success."
