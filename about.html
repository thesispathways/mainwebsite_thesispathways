Our Mission

At ThesisPathways, our mission is to empower students to pursue original research, develop academic skills, and gain a competitive edge for college, graduate, or medical school. We connect students with top university mentors who guide every step of the process.

How We Work

Students are paired with experienced PhD mentors from leading universities, including Ivy Leagues. Mentors provide hands-on guidance through research projects, helping students learn, publish, and showcase their work.

ThesisPathways was founded by a PhD graduate from Yale University. She started ThesisPathways out of a passion for guiding curious students. Inspired by her own research journey, she wanted to create a program where high school and undergraduate students could explore original research, receive personalized mentorship, and develop skills that open doors to college and meaningful careers. Her mission is to help every student realize their academic potential and discover their path to success.
