Program Pricing

Our 20-session, one-on-one mentorship program costs:

  • Standard Price: $2,675
  • Students can apply to the program for free. Payment is only required after the student has been accepted and officially enrolls in the program.

Scholarships

ThesisPathways offers need-based scholarships to support students with financial need. To apply:

  1. Submit the standard Student Application first.
  2. After completing the standard application, submit the Scholarship Application.

Both full and partial (half price) scholarships are offered at ThesisPathways. Our goal is to mentor at least 10 scholarship students per year.
