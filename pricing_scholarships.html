Scholarships

ThesisPathways offers need-based scholarships for low-income students to make research mentorship accessible.

Eligibility: Students with demonstrated financial need.

  1. Submit the standard Student Application first.
  2. Then submit the Scholarship Application.

Both full and partial (half-price) scholarships are offered. Our goal is to mentor at least 10 scholarship students per year.
