Potential Research Topics

Explore a wide variety of research areas available through ThesisPathways.

STEM

Astronomy / Space Science
Explore the universe, planets, stars, and galaxies, and study the fundamental laws of physics that govern space.

Biology
Investigate living organisms, ecosystems, genetics, and the interactions between species and their environments.

Biomedical
Research topics include human health, disease mechanisms, and the development of medical therapies and technologies.

Data Science / AI
Analyze large datasets, develop machine learning models, and explore artificial intelligence applications in science and society.

Engineering
Study and design solutions in mechanical, civil, electrical, or software engineering and innovation projects.

Environmental Science
Investigate ecosystems, climate change, conservation, and the impact of human activity on the planet.

Math / Statistics
Explore pure and applied mathematics, probability, statistics, and analytical modeling techniques.

Public Health / Epidemiology
Study disease patterns, population health, and strategies to improve public health outcomes.

Social Sciences

Criminology
Examine crime, law enforcement, and the social factors influencing criminal behavior.

Economics
Study markets, finance, resource allocation, and the economic forces that shape societies.

Media and Communication Studies
Analyze media, digital communication, and the impact of technology on society and culture.

Political Science
Explore governance, policy, political behavior, and international relations.

Psychology
Study human behavior, cognition, emotions, and mental health through scientific research.

Humanities

History
Investigate historical events, cultures, and societies to understand how the past shapes the present.

Music / Art History
Study artistic movements, composers, and visual arts across different cultures and eras.

Philosophy
Explore fundamental questions about ethics, knowledge, reality, and human thought.

The possibilities are limitless! We are always adding new mentors with unique expertise!
