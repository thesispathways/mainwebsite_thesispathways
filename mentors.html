Why Mentor with ThesisPathways?

ThesisPathways mentors play a critical role in shaping the next generation of researchers, clinicians, and scholars. Our mentors work one-on-one with high school and undergraduate students to guide them through original research or literature review projects that culminate in a journal publication.

  • Make a meaningful educational impact through personalized mentorship
  • Work with motivated, high-achieving students
  • Flexible, fully remote mentorship
  • Paid opportunities with optional volunteer scholarship mentorship
  • Gain teaching, mentorship, and leadership experience

Mentor Role & Responsibilities

As a ThesisPathways mentor, you will:

  • Meet one-on-one with students for 20 structured 30-minute sessions
  • Guide students in developing research questions and project design
  • Provide feedback on outlines, drafts, and revisions
  • Ensure research is ethical, feasible, and safe for independent student work
  • Support students through the journal submission and revision process
  • Communicate exclusively through approved platforms
  • Optionally provide letters of recommendation

Qualifications

  • PhD or MD holder, or PhD/MD candidate
  • Affiliated with or graduated from a Top 50 U.S. university (U.S. News & World Report)
  • Experience conducting academic research
  • Strong communication and mentorship skills
  • Commitment to academic integrity and student safety
  • Ability to commit to scheduled sessions and milestone reviews

Mentorship Structure

ThesisPathways provides a highly structured mentorship framework to support both mentors and students. Milestones ensure steady progress and high-quality outcomes.

Early Sessions

Project brainstorming, research question development, and feasibility review

Mid Program

Outline development, data collection or literature synthesis, first draft writing

Final Phase

Final manuscript preparation, journal submission, and response to reviewer feedback

Support for Mentors

  • Comprehensive mentor guide and onboarding materials
  • Clear milestone reminders and administrative support
  • All sessions automatically recorded for safety and transparency
  • Dedicated administrative oversight for scheduling and payments
